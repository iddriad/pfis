Predictive Fraud Intelligence System v1.0

Monitors mobile money transactions in real-time across 6 user profiles using a weighted rule-based engine with 6 detection hurdles.

Transactions are scored 0–100 and classified as Safe, Moderate, or High Risk based on configurable thresholds.
